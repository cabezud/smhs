Come catch up with the Class of 1995. Kick off the weekend on Friday with casual cocktails at the Langham Tap Room (aka the Bar at the old Huntington Hotel).

Then Saturday, it’s the main event…the 20th Reunion at Brookside Golf Club. Come for the old school music, come for dinner and the OPEN BAR, come for the nametag with your high school picture on it – but most of come to see your old classmates!

Sunday its Family Day at Lacy Park. Having a hangover is no excuse - come and enjoy some fun in the sun with the family. Pizza from the fabulous Tony’s Pizza will be served!

Send us your pictures!
